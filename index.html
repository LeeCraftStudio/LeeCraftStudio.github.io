About Lee Craft Studio

Founded in 2023, Lee Craft Studio is a small, independent workshop dedicated to creating beautiful, functional handmade goods. Each piece is crafted with care using sustainable materials and traditional techniques.

We believe that objects used in daily life should bring joy and be made to last. That's why we pay attention to every detail, from material selection to finishing touches.
